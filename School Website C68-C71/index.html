TEACHING

Affiliated with CISCE, New Delhi, Hutchings High School, Pune offers schooling from Pre Nursery to class XII. The evaluation system includes unit tests, assessments and examination with English given the utmost priority. Students are encouraged to participate in co curricular activities such as debates, dramatics, elocution, art and craft exhibition, science exhibition, quiz and spell bee. Sporting activities include basketball, archery, badminton, sports yoga, athletics, karate, football and swimming.
